Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application FactoringPractice (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App does not provide user registration features, and we do not directly collect your name, address, phone number, email address, or other contact information through the App.

However, the App uses third-party services for advertising and consent management. These services may collect or use information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage information
  • Information related to ad impressions, clicks, and ad delivery
  • Consent status information

If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and any other information you choose to provide in order to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • To serve ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud
  • To manage user consent choices
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or platform requirements

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements.

Google may collect and use information such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, app usage information, and ad interaction information for purposes including ad delivery, ad performance measurement, fraud prevention, and showing more relevant ads.

For more information about how Google handles information, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services

4. Consent Management and Tracking

The App may use the Google User Messaging Platform SDK to manage consent choices related to advertising.

On iOS, if access to the advertising identifier for tracking purposes is required, the App will request permission under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change your tracking permission at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Depending on your region and settings, the App may show an in-app privacy or consent options screen.

5. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell or disclose personal information to third parties without your consent, except where required or permitted by law.

However, third-party services used in the App may collect and use information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

The App does not store user account information on our own servers.

If you contact us, we may retain your contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for reasonable record-keeping purposes. When it is no longer needed, we will delete it appropriately.

For information collected by third-party services, please refer to the privacy policies and data retention practices of those service providers.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed primarily to children. If a user who is under the age requiring parental consent under applicable law uses the App, they should do so with the consent of a parent or guardian.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without required parental consent, we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective when the updated Privacy Policy is posted on this page.
